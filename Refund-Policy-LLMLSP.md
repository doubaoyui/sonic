### Refund Policy (LLMLSP)

This Refund Policy explains when refunds may be issued for purchases made on https://llmlsp.com.

LLMLSP provides prepaid balance ("Credits") that can be used to pay for usage within the Service.

- **1 Credit = 1 USD** for accounting and billing purposes within the Service.
- Credits are consumed as you use the Service. Usage costs depend on the model and may change over time.

### 1. General rule

Unless required by applicable law, **consumed Credits are not refundable**.

### 2. Refunds for unused balance

We may, at our discretion, issue a refund for the **unused portion** of your prepaid balance if all of the following are true:

- The payment is confirmed as successful by our payment processor (e.g., Stripe).
- The requested refund amount does not exceed your current **unused** Credit balance.
- There is no evidence of fraud, abuse, or chargeback risk.

If a refund is approved:

- The corresponding Credits will be deducted from your balance.
- The refund will be processed back to the original payment method where possible.

### 3. Non-refundable situations (examples)

Refunds may be refused for situations including, but not limited to:

- Credits already consumed by usage
- Suspicious activity, policy violations, or abuse
- Attempts to obtain refunds while continuing to use the Service with the same balance
- Chargebacks, disputes, or payments flagged by the payment processor

### 4. Chargebacks and disputes

If you file a chargeback or payment dispute with your payment provider:

- We may suspend or restrict your account and access to the Service during the investigation.
- If a chargeback is successful, we may deduct the equivalent Credits from your balance or take other reasonable steps to prevent loss.

### 5. How to request a refund

Contact us at **support@llmlsp.com** with:

- The email used for your account
- The payment receipt / transaction reference (if available)
- The reason for your request

### 6. Processing time

We will review your request as soon as reasonably possible. If approved, the actual time for funds to appear depends on your payment method and payment processor.
